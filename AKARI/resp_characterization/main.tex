\section{Introduction}


Many of the effects described in this work have been discussed in \citet{verdugo_akari_2007}. Many of the results there were determined using Slow-Scan data, but have not been characterized in the all-sky survey data. These effects are clearly present to some extent in all AKARI FIS data, but not characterized well enough to be removed.

Many of these effects are quite subtle, and are difficult to see in full-sky maps. One of the main advantages of the Commander framework is the conditioning on a given model. In this framework, unmodeled effects become much easier to spot and characterize.
