   \titlerunning{\Cosmoglobe DR3: AKARI low-level preprocessing}
   \authorrunning{E.~Gjerl{\o}w et al.}

   \date{\today} 
   
   \abstract{We present the suite of preprocessing analysis performed on the raw AKARI low-level time-ordered data to convert from ADU to flux units. The procedure described in this paper corresponds to the Green Box processing pipeline of the original AKARI analysis, but with several important differences. The preprocessing pipeline includes digital units-to-volt conversions, cryogenic readout electronics (CRE) non-linearity corrections, flagging of bad data, glitch corrections, and scan data calibration.}
